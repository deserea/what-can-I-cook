# 🍽️ What Can I Cook?

A powerful cooking assistant for American home kitchens that helps you decide what to cook based on the ingredients you already have.

---

## 🧠 What This App Does

Instead of searching for recipes manually, this app:

- Matches your ingredients to real recipes
- Suggests what you can cook **right now**
- Shows what you're missing
- Helps you upgrade meals to **chef-level**
- Reduces food waste
- Supports beginner → advanced cooks

---

## 🔥 Features

### 🥕 Ingredient-Based Cooking
- Enter ingredients you **have**
- Optionally consider ingredients you **might have**
- Get suggestions for ingredients to **buy**

Every recipe contains:

- Required ingredients
- Optional add-ons
- Ingredient alternatives (substitutions)
- Instructions (step-by-step)

- Cooking tips
- Questions to guide the cook
